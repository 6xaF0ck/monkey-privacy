Privacy Policy  |  Last updated: March 13, 2026

🎟️ Entertainment App  •  All Ages  •  Fictional Trading

Inspired by Burton Malkiel’s "A Random Walk Down Wall Street," Monkey with a Dart is a stock market simulator designed to test if professional strategy can beat random chance. We value your privacy and keep your data on your device.

1. Nature of the Service (Disclaimers)

  • Fictional Currency: All virtual dollars and balances within the App are strictly for simulation and have no real-world monetary value.
  • No Real Trading: This App does not facilitate real-world stock trading, brokerage services, or financial transactions.
  • Not Financial Advice: Data is for educational purposes only and should not be used for real-world investment decisions.

2. Information We Collect

Simply put: None. All data created within the app—including your portfolio name, stock selections, and performance history—is stored exclusively on your device. We never see it, and it is never transmitted to our servers.

3. Stock Price Data

The app fetches publicly available stock price data from third-party financial providers to display current and historical prices. These requests are made directly from your device and contain no personally identifiable information.

4. Third-Party Services

The App is distributed via the Google Play Store and utilizes Google Play Services for basic performance monitoring. We do not use third-party advertising IDs or track your location.

5. User Rights & Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all information associated with the app at any time by simply uninstalling the application or clearing the app cache in your device settings.
