Privacy Policy  ·  Last updated: March 5, 2026

📱 Entertainment App  ·  All Ages  ·  No Data Collected

Monkey with a Dart does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. All your data stays on your device. Always.

1. Information We Collect

Simply put — none. All data created within the app including your portfolio name, stock selections, allocations, and performance history is stored exclusively on your device. We never see it.

We do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Financial account information
  • Usage analytics or crash reports

2. Stock Price Data

The app fetches publicly available stock price data from third-party financial data providers (including Yahoo Finance) to display current and historical prices. These requests are made directly from your device and contain no personally identifiable information.

3. Fictional Investment Disclaimer

All portfolios and investments within Monkey with a Dart are entirely fictional. The app uses a simulated starting capital of $100,000 for entertainment and educational purposes only. Nothing in this app constitutes financial advice.

4. Children's Privacy

The app is suitable for all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information from any users, there are no special provisions required for users under the age of 13. No data is transmitted from the app to us or any third party.

5. Third-Party Services

The app retrieves stock price information from publicly available financial data APIs solely to display market data. No user information is shared with these services.

6. Data Storage & Security

All app data is stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us. If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data is permanently deleted.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated date at the top of this page.
